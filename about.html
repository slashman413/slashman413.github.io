About AI Toolkit

AI Toolkit (slashman413.github.io) is an independent, one-person project that builds and maintains a growing collection of free, fast, privacy-friendly online tools. Everything is free to use, needs no sign-up, and most tools run entirely in your browser — the data you enter never leaves your device.

What we make

  • AI helpers — LLM VRAM calculator, token cost calculator, AI image-size calculator, prompt library.
  • Developer tools — JSON formatter & regex tester, Base64/JWT/UUID/hash utilities, color tools.
  • Everyday utilities — password generator, word counter, unit converter, QR code generator, image compressor, PDF tools, calculators, Pomodoro timer.
  • Finance & data dashboards — Taiwan ETF analysis, stock screener & backtesting, a compound-interest calculator, and a 3D global-events tracker.

Why these tools?

Most online utilities are cluttered with pop-ups, demand uploads, or hide features behind sign-ups. The goal here is the opposite: clean, single-purpose tools that load instantly, work on mobile, support multiple languages, and respect your privacy by doing the work locally whenever possible.

How it's built

The tools are hand-built static web apps. Where a tool processes your input (text, files, images), that processing happens client-side in your browser. The finance dashboards are generated from public market data and refreshed automatically.
