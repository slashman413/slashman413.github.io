Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-21

This Privacy Policy explains how the AI content-automation service ("we", "us") handles information. The service is operated by an individual to publish AI-generated videos to the operator's own connected social accounts.

1. Information We Use

  • Platform access tokens / login credentials that you (the operator) explicitly provide to authorize publishing (e.g. OAuth tokens for YouTube/TikTok, login cookies for Bilibili). These are stored as encrypted secrets and used only to upload content to your own accounts.
  • Publicly available trending topics retrieved from public sources (e.g. Google Trends) to choose video subjects.

2. How We Use Information

Access tokens are used solely to publish content to the connected accounts you authorized. We do not sell, rent, or share your credentials or personal data with third parties.

3. Data Storage & Security

Credentials are stored as encrypted secrets (e.g. GitHub Actions encrypted secrets) and are never exposed in logs or to third parties. Tokens can be revoked at any time by the operator from the respective platform.

4. Third-Party Services

The service interacts with third-party APIs (YouTube, TikTok, Bilibili, Google Trends, DeepSeek, image-generation services). Your use of those platforms is governed by their own privacy policies.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Credentials are retained only while the automation is active and are deleted when you remove the corresponding secret or revoke access on the platform. To request deletion, revoke the platform token or contact us below.

6. Children's Privacy

The service is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their data.

7. Changes

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date will reflect changes.
