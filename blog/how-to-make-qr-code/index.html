What can a QR code contain?

  • A website link (URL) — the most common use.
  • Plain text — a message, code or note.
  • Wi-Fi credentials — guests scan to join your network, no typing.
  • Contact details — phone, email or a vCard.

Do QR codes expire?

Static QR codes don't expire. A static code encodes your content directly into the pattern, so it works forever and has no scan limits or tracking. (Some commercial "dynamic" QR services add an expiring redirect — you don't need that for a simple link.)

Tips for QR codes that always scan

  • Test before you print — scan it with two different phones.
  • Keep good contrast — dark code on a light background.
  • Don't print too small — aim for at least 2 × 2 cm; bigger for distant scanning (posters).
  • Leave a quiet zone — keep clear margin around the code.
